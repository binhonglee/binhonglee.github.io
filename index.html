About me

Bin Hong is currently a student at Arizona State University, majoring in Software Engineering. His interest in programming has been cultivated since high school, competing in numerous robotics competitions as a team programmer. At age 16, he had made his dream, and the dream of many others, by winning the gold medal in the 2010 World Robot Olympiad. He had also served as a part time tutor for elementary school students in hopes that they would one day shine brightly in the world of software engineering and robotics. As soon as his tertiary education started, Bin Hong, as the founder and president, recruited people who share the same interests and formed the Robotics Club at INTI International College. Outside of college, he is also involved in events such as the Global Game Jam to make use of his experience in programming to venture into the gaming field. Currently, he is working at Humanity X as a Director or Product Development & Outreach. Along with his partners, he believed that by combining technology with humanity causes, it would help spread and retain the humanity values and awareness in people.
